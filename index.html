Crown Hotel was established in 1975 is one of the oldest hotels in Port Moresby with a rich history of operating under various names and organizations.
For the past five years, it has proudly been known as Crown Hotel.
This long-standing legacy sets us apart, making Crown Hotel unique in the competitive hospitality landscape.
