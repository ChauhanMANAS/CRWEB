01 Our hotel

The Crown Hotel

Crown Hotel, established in 1975, is one of the oldest hotels in Port Moresby (POM), with a rich history of operating under various names and organizations. For the past five years, it has proudly been known as Crown Hotel. This long-standing legacy sets us apart, making Crown Hotel unique in the competitive hospitality landscape.
As a recipient of the "Best Heritage Hotel" award, Crown Hotel beautifully combines heritage and modernity, offering guests a truly authentic experience. The hotel's architecture and ambiance evoke a sense of history, while still providing the comforts and services expected of a contemporary hotel.
